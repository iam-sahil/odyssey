## Key Objectives

1. **Vercel OSS Program** — visibility, previews, OSS credibility.
2. **Multi-primitive coverage** — drop-in animated behaviors for popular primitive libraries.
3. **Animate UI proprietary components** — strong UX, composable APIs, reusable motion presets.
4. **Animated Lucide icons** — consistent, production-ready variants.
5. **Blocks** & **Templates** — accelerate composition and adoption.
